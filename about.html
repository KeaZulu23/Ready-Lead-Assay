About Ready Lead Assay Laboratory

Ready Lead Assay Laboratory is a premier service provider in the gold mining industry, delivering high-quality lab analysis and testing services for nearly two decades. Our expertise lies in providing accurate, efficient, and innovative gold assaying services, helping mining companies make informed decisions in their quest for gold. With a robust commitment to precision and innovation, we offer a comprehensive suite of gold testing services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients in the mining sector.

Our Activities

Internal Audit

Internal audit is a systematic and independent examination of an organization's activities and processes to evaluate their effectiveness, compliance with policies and regulations, and adherence to established procedures.

Proficiency Testing in Laboratory

Proficiency testing involves the evaluation of a laboratory's performance by comparing its test results to those of other laboratories using the same methods and samples.

Risk Assessment

Risk assessment involves identifying, analyzing, and evaluating potential risks and hazards associated with laboratory activities, processes, and operations.

Method Validation

Method validation involves assessing the suitability, accuracy, and reliability of analytical methods used in laboratories. This process ensures that the methods employed are appropriate for the intended use, produce accurate results, and meet regulatory requirements.
