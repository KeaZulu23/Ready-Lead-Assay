Our Services

Carbon Analysis

Our carbon analysis service meticulously evaluates the carbon content in samples. This analysis is crucial for assessing carbonaceous ore deposits and optimizing gold extraction processes, helping our clients improve recovery rates and operational efficiency.

Bullion Analysis

We provide precise assessment of bullion samples to ensure compliance with industry standards. Our bullion analysis service gives our clients confidence in the quality and purity of their precious metals, essential for market transactions and quality assurance.

Bottle Roll Testing

Our bottle roll testing service simulates leaching conditions to evaluate the leaching potential of ores. This test provides valuable insights into the leaching kinetics and helps optimize gold extraction processes, ultimately enhancing the efficiency of mining operations.

Solids Analysis

We offer accurate assessment of solid materials, essential for understanding the mineral composition and quality in mining operations. Our solids analysis service helps clients make informed decisions regarding ore processing and resource management.
